Place the peaches and the sugar in a small food processor and process until broken down. Pour into a small pot and bring to a boil on medium-high heat. Once the mixture comes to a boil, remove from heat. Transfer into a small bowl and refrigerate 1 hour to cool. Meanwhile, skin and deseed lychee if using fresh. Add the milk and peach mixture and lychee to a high powered blender and blend until combined.
Serve immediately.
