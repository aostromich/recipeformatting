Lychee Peachy Milk
Yield: 2 servings | Prep Time: 25 min | Total Time: 1 hr 25 min
  • Whole lychee or 1/2 cup lychee juice
  • 1 cup peaches, sliced (2 medium)
  • 1 1/2 - 2 cups milk of choice (depending on how much peach flavour you want)
Place the peaches and the sugar in a small food processor and process until broken down
Pour into a small pot and bring to a boil on medium-high heat
Once the mixture comes to a boil, remove from heat
Transfer into a small bowl and refrigerate 1 hour to cool
Meanwhile, skin and deseed lychee if using fresh
Add the milk and peach mixture and lychee to a high powered blender and blend until combined
Serve immediately

To serve: Serve immediately
